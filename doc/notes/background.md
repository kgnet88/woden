## [Names of Odin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_names_of_Odin)

* Odin, Woden, Wodan, Wuotan, Wotan
* Hrafnagud - Raven God 
* Fjolnir - Wise One, concealer
* Fjolsvid, Fjolsvin - Very Wise
* Gangari, Ganglari, Gangleri - Wanderer / Wayweary

* Svidur - Wise One
